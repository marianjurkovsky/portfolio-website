About

My Story

I'm Marian Jurkovsky, a UI/UX Designer & product person passionate about designing innovative yet straightforward visuals and experiences. I enjoy solving problems with inspiration from real-life stories. My favorite aspect is taking complex processes and user journeys then translating them into simple and easy-to-use tech. With my background in civil engineering, I prefer to make designs with sharp shapes and high visibility. I am still learning new things with new projects.

Helping people has always been at the core of what I do. Working in the hospitality industry most of my life made me be a good listener and helped improve my problem-solving abilities for good. When I'm away from my work desk, I love being outdoors - walking by the river, cycle around town, traveling to learn about world history, or sitting with friends or family in a beer garden.
